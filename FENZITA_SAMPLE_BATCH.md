## Why This Order

- Start with short fiction to stabilize sentence-level style
- Move to a core series piece
- Then stress-test localization-heavy prose
- Then handle classical adaptation
- End with the longest nonfiction piece once terminology is steadier

## Batch 1 Deliverables

- English title
- English body
- `translationKey`
- English taxonomy labels where needed
- Notes on terms that should be added to the glossary

## Known Risks

- `键上话` may require the most intervention at sentence level
- `译异录` may require a different register from `醍醐堂记`
- `水浒` essay may need paragraph restructuring rather than literal transfer
