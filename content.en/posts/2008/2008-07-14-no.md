A: Boss, someone's here to see you. Want to meet him?

B: Nay.

A: He's here to return the car he borrowed last time. How much should we charge him?

B: None.

A: But he insists on leaving twenty thousand yuan.

B: Nope.

A: Fine. Besides that, he's brought more goods. What did you think of the last batch?

B: Bad.

A: He says this batch is the purest of all.

B: Crooked.

A: He also brought you a girl. Want to look at the photo?

B: Tiny.

A: He asks what kind you prefer.

B: No.

A: All right. I understand. Then I'll send him away. Anything else you want to add?

B: Negatory.

A: He asks whether you've left him any message.

B: Absolutely not!

A: He also asks...

B: No! Why the hell are you so damned wordy? You're driving me insane!
