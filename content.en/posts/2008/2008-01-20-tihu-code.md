**How to play [tihu code]**

[Tihu Cipher (tihu code)] is a web-based puzzle game. Players need to make flexible use of online tools and different modes of thought. As long as you can find the address leading to the next stage, you have cleared the level. The key may test common knowledge, may be no more than a spot-the-difference game, or may require some calculation. The process of thinking through the puzzle cannot be measured by ordinary logic. It may be a brainteaser, it may be a serious problem for study, or the answer may be right under your nose.
